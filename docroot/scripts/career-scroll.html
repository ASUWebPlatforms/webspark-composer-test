Our students' career journey starts before the first day of law school.

ASU Law provides you with the online Lawfit Career Assessment, which helps you identify different career paths that may be of interest.

Create a foundation for career success in your first year through hands-on resources including resume reviews, interview prep and job fairs.

  • Foundations Professional Development Seminar
  • One-on-one counseling
  • Resume and cover letter review
  • Online job board and directories
  • Pro-bono opportunities

Gain real-world, paid and unpaid practical experience as a 2L and 3L through a variety of rewarding externship opportunities.

  • Judicial, government and public interest externships
  • Paid externships with for-profit entities
  • Domestic and international spring semester externships
  • Federal advocacy and regulatory externships (in Washington, D.C.)
  • Internal Rule of Law and Security Program (in Washington, D.C.)
